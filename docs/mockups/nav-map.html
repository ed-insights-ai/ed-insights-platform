What each page needs to answer

⚡ War Room — "How is my season going?"
→ Record, form, conference position
→ Am I trending up or down? (last 5 games)
→ Who is performing / who is slumping?
→ What's the next threat I'm preparing for?
→ Any anomalies I should know about?
📅 Schedule — "What happened / what's coming?"
→ Full season timeline, past results
→ Home vs away record at a glance
→ Conference vs non-conference split
→ Upcoming opponents + their current form
→ Click any game → Game Detail
👤 Roster — "Who is doing what?"
→ Every player with their season stats
→ Per-90 rates (not just raw totals)
→ Sort by any metric instantly
→ Minutes distribution (who is seeing the field)
→ Click player → Player Profile
📊 Conference Overview — "Where do we fit?"
→ Full standings with GD, form, PPG
→ Who are the top scorers conference-wide?
→ Which teams are on hot/cold streaks?
→ How do teams compare on key metrics?
→ Click team → drill into their War Room
📈 Analytics — "What are our patterns?"
→ Goals scored/allowed per game (trend line)
→ Home vs away performance split
→ Conference vs non-conference split
→ Multi-season comparison (are we improving?)
→ Shot funnel (shots → SOG → goals)
🃏 Player Profile — "Who is this player?"
→ Season + career stats table
→ Per-90 KPIs vs conference avg
→ Radar chart (percentile rank vs GAC)
→ Game log (when did they show up?)
→ Multi-season trajectory (developing?)
